Underline

An underline, also called an underscore, is a line immediately below a portion of text.

Syntax

<u>

HDITA

<u>

MDITA (core and extended profiles)

There is no specific support in MDITA core profile. If needed, use an HDITA snippet.

Attributes

The following attributes are available on this element: localization attributes, @class, @keyref, and @outputclass.
