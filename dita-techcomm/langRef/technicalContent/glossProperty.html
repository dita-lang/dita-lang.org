<glossProperty>

The <glossProperty> element specifies additional details about the preferred term or its subject, such as the gender of a noun.

Specialization hierarchy

The <glossProperty> element is specialized from <data>. It is defined in the glossary entry module.

Attributes

The following attributes are available on this element: data-element attributes, link-relationship attributes, and universal attributes.

</glossentry>
