<frontmatter>

The <frontmatter> element contains the material that precedes the main body of a document.

Usage information

The front matter might include items such as an abstract, a preface, and various types of book lists such as a <toc>, <tablelist>, or <figurelist>.

Specialization hierarchy

The <frontmatter> element is specialized from <topicref>. It is defined in the bookmap module.
